Lemon Market

Two players: Bidder and Owner

Roles

-The Owner rejects or accepts the bid.
-The Bidder make bids to purchase the commodity.

Value

-The owner knows the value of the commodity to be bidded for, the bidder knows only the range of possible values.

Payoff(rejection)

-If bid amount is less than the value of commodity, the owner rejects and no purchase. Your payoff is 0.00.

Payoff(acceptance)

-If bid greater or equal than value of commodity. The owner accepts and purchase is made.
Payoff is 1.5 x the value minus bid amount i.e (1.5 x value - bid amount).

In each round your profit will be equal to:

Profit = (Price - Quality) x Market share

The social contribution associated to your production at each round of the game will be equal to:

Social contribution = 1.5 x Quality x Market share

The points you earn in the paying period, both in terms of profit and social contribution, will be transformed in cash.

The Dictator Game consists of two participants: The Dictator and Other Participant. The Dictator is given an amount of money.The Other Participant is given nothing. The Dictator is told that he must offer some amount of that money to the Other Participant, even if that amount is zero. Whatever amount the Dictator offers to the Other Participant must be accepted.

In Private Value Auction Game, Participants anonymously paired.

Each of the participants will submit a bid for a prize being sold in an auction.

Before bidding, you will be given a 'prize value' which is the money value to you for the item being auctioned. Prize values, which will be determined randomly.

Based on the prize value given, each participant will make a 'bid' and neither of the participants will be able to see the other participant's bid.

After bidding the person with the highest bid will purchase the item being sold. In the event of a tie bid, the winner will be randomly chosen. The winner's payoff will be given by prize value minus their own bid. The other bidder (looser) will earn nothing.

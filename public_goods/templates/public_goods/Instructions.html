{% blocktrans trimmed with endowment=Constants.endowment %} In this study, you will be randomly allocated to a group of 3 or 4 participants. Each participant is given {{ endowment }}. {% blocktrans trimmed with efficiency=Constants.efficiency_factor %} The earnings from the project are calculated as follows: The contributions of all participants are added up, the total contribution is multiplied by a factor of {{ efficiency }}, and the resulting amount is the total earnings from the project, which is evenly split among all participants.

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Suppose in a group, 1 participant contributed 80 points, 1 participant contributed 40, and 1 participant contributed nothing. What would be the payoff of the participant who contributed 80 points? {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Solution: 92 points {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Explanation: Total contribution from all participants was 80+40+0=120. Total earnings from the project were 120*1.8=216. Each participant got an equal share of the earnings, that is, 216/3=72. Hence, payoff for the participant who contributed 80 points was 100-80+72=92. {% endblocktrans %}

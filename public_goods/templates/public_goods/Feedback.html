Suppose in a group, 1 participant contributes 80 points, 1 participant contributes 40, and 1 participant contributes nothing. What is the payoff of the participant who contributed 80 points?

Solution: 92 points

Explanation: Total contribution from all participants is 80+40+0=120. Total earnings from the project are 120*1.8=216. Each participant gets an equal share of the earnings, that is, 216/3=72. Hence, payoff for the participant who contributed 80 points is 100-80+72=92.

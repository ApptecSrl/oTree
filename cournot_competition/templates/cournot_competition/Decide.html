You have been randomly and anonymously assigned to a group with {{ num_other_players }} other players. Each player will represent a company. All companies manufacture the same type of product.

Each company decides simultaneously and independently how many units of the product to manufacture. In this market, all produced units are sold, but the more that is produced, the lower the unit price will be. Furthermore, the total production capacity is {{ total_capacity }}, each company can therefore produce at most {{ max_units_per_player }} units.

The unit price, measured in points, is calculated as follows:

    Unit price = {{ total_capacity }} – Units produced by your company – Units produced by the other {{ num_other_players }} companies

Once the unit price is obtained, your profit (also measured in points) is then calculated as follows:

    Profit = Unit price × Units produced by you

For every profit point, you will receive {{ currency_per_point }}.

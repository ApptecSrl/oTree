Suppose that participant A sent 20 points to participant B. Having received X points, participant B sent 50 points to participant A. In the end, participant A had Y points. What are X and Y respectively?

Solution: X=60 and Y=130.

Explanation: Participant A first sent 20 points to participant B. It was tripled thus participant B received 20*3=60 points, that is X equal 60. Then participant B sent 50 points to participant A. Participant A received exactly 50 points. Hence at last participant A had 100-20+50=130 points, that is Y equal 130.

Rules

Player A will propose a contract to Player B. The contract includes two figures that must be chosen as follows:

  • Fixed payment, which can be chosen as any number from at least –{{ fixed_payment }} to at most {{ fixed_payment }}
  • Return share, which can be chosen as a multiple of 10%, that is 10%, 20%, … , 90%, or 100%

Note that a negative fixed payment means that Player B will have to pay the amount to Player A.

Once Player A has submitted the contract, Player B must decides whether to accept or to reject it.

If rejected:

  • Player A will receive {{ reject_principal_pay }}
  • Player B will receive {{ reject_agent_pay }}

If accepted, Player B then chooses a level of effort, which can be any whole number from at least 1 to at most 10. Effort exertion is costly only to Player B. The exerted level of effort determines the total return, which is to be apportioned according to the proposed return share.

  • Player A will receive:
    (100% – [B's return share in %]) × [total return] – [B's fixed payment]
  • Player B will receive:
    [B's return share in %] × [total return] + [B's fixed payment] – [B's effort cost]
